Chicken Egg Katsudon

Homepage

Description

Influenced by Western Cuisine, this Japanese dish is packed with protein and calories that are sure to leave anyone's stomach full after a few servings.

Preparation and Cooking time is around an hour or so. So let's listen to music or a podcast and make this delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs (6 in total)
  • Water
  • Chicken
  • Dashi, Mirin, Soy Sauce, Sugar (any kind)
  • Onions, Spring Onions
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Mix in the flour and eggs to create a simple batter
  2. Clean and cut the Chicken to your liking (don't make them too thick otherwise the heat will take ages to get through)
  3. Dip the cut Chicken into the batter, then cover them with the breadcrumbs
  4. Place into a preheated pan with any kind of oil & cook into golden brown
    5. Now we've prepped the chicken, let's start making the actual Katsudon!
  5. Pour water into a pan and heat until semi-boiling
  6. Add in the Dashi, Mirin, Soy sauce and Sugar to your tastebuds' liking, but make sure that the Dashi has always the bigger amount
  7. Chop up the Onions and Spring Onions and place into the pan, stir and let them cook for about five minutes or so
  8. Add in the Katsu Chicken you've just made
  9. Crack the remaining five eggs into a bowl & mix until their semi-mixed.
  10. Pour into the Pan, let it simmer on a medium heat for 6 minutes or so
  11. Cook up some Rice and enjoy!
