Protein Miso

Homepage

Description

This tantalizingly warm broth will also warm you up, while providing you with the right amount of nutrients that you need to take on the day!

This meal will take around 10 minutes to make - the perfect replacement for those cold, sugar-filled cereals!

Ingredients

  • Miso Paste
  • Dashi
  • Wakame (Dried Seaweed)
  • Spring Onions
  • Tofu
  • Bacon
  • Eggs (I'd go for two)

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water, then bring it to a boil
  2. Cut up the Bacon (add as much as you like) and add them in
  3. After a few seconds, add in the chopped up spring onions and dried Wakame
  4. Then add in chopped Tofu
    5. Let the broth boil for a few minutes...
  5. Turn down the heat and add in the Dashi powder (To your liking)
  6. Grab around three-quarters of a tablespoon of Miso Paste and gently dissolve it into the broth with the help of a fork
  7. Once that is done, bring the pot to a boil again (but a little lower than last time)
  8. Crack and mix the eggs, then add them into the pot
  9. After 30 seconds, the Protein Miso Soup should be Ready!
