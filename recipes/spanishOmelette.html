Spanish Omelette

Homepage

Description

This traditional dish from the Iberian Peninsula is known for its's many varieties and alterations, meaning this is a perfectly customisable dish which you can change to you liking!

Prep and Cooking time (for my version) is around an hour

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Mushrooms
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Pour a generous amount of Olive Oil into a pan, put it on a high heat
  2. Add in the chopped bacon
  3. Add in chopped Potatoes and Onions
  4. Add in chopped Tomatoes and Onions
  5. Put a lid on and let it simmer on a medium high heat. Periodically check to see if the Potatoes are thoroughly cooked
  6. Once confident mixture is cooked, add in some beaten eggs and once again simmer for around 6 minutes or so
  7. Once it looks cooked, plate it up and enjoy!
